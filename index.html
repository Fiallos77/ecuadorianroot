Who We Are

BORN FROM ECUADORIAN PRIDE

Ecuadorian Roots Dance Group is a folkloric dance group founded in Brisbane, Australia. Our mission: to share the richness of Ecuadorian culture with the multicultural Australian community.

We represent the 4 regions of Ecuador — Sierra, Coast, Amazon and Galápagos — through their most authentic dances: Sanjuanito, Bomba del Chota, Amorfino and Shuar dances.

We are preparing for our official debut on June 20, 2026 at Inti Raymi — Multicultural Australia. A historic moment for our community in Brisbane!

The 4 Regions of Ecuador

OUR IDENTITY

🏔️
Sierra
Sanjuanito · Albazo · Danzante
The Andes with red ponchos, straw hats and the unmistakable joy of the Sanjuanito — Ecuador's national rhythm.
🌊
Coast
Amorfino · Alza · Pasacalle
The vibrant montubio culture of Ecuador's Pacific coast — spontaneous verse, song and dance all in one.
🌿
Amazon
Shuar Dance · Kichwa Dance
The Ecuadorian Amazon with colourful feathers, chonta spears and the deep spirit of the rainforest.
🏝️
Galápagos
Bomba del Chota · Pasillo
The unique island culture of the Enchanted Islands — where the coastal pasillo meets the spirit of the sea.
